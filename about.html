About Us

A cozy café experience in Cabagan.

Goodman Café is a local coffee shop concept created for customers who enjoy affordable drinks, sweet snacks, and a relaxing café atmosphere.

The café offers coffee, iced coffee, milk tea, non-coffee beverages, and cookies that are perfect for students, friends, families, and coffee lovers.

Why Choose Goodman Café?

Modern Café Style

Goodman Café uses a warm wine red and cream visual identity to create a simple, elegant, and modern café feel.

Affordable Favorites

The menu includes coffee, milk tea, non-coffee drinks, and cookies that match the taste of young customers and casual café visitors.

Customer-Friendly

The website includes menu browsing, an order form, a feedback form, and contact information for easy customer access.

Business Description

Goodman Café is a refreshment and snack business that serves coffee-based drinks, milk tea, non-coffee beverages, and cookies. This web application is designed to help customers view the menu, submit orders, send feedback, and locate the café easily.
